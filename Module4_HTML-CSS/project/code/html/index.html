Welcome to Tachette

Self care, Task management, To do

What is Tachette?

Tachette is a new kind of task management app. Its aim is to combine self-care with traditional to-do.

Examples of To-Do Tasks:
  • work project
  • school assignment
  • personal errand
  • household chore
Examples of Self-Care Tasks:
  • get out of bed
  • shower
  • make a meal
  • do something fun

Why Tachette?

Because people have both good and bad days. They have days where they have the energy to tackle traditional to-do tasks, and days where they need to put those things aside to replenish themselves through the self-care tasks. In the experience of the creators of Tachette, two separate apps are needed to juggle the To-Do and Self-Care tasks. This can lead to rumination and negative thought cycles

Example: John Doe starts off the week really well. He crushes his To-Do lists for Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday. But, when Thursday hits, he hits a wall. Perhaps he struggles with depression in general, and this Thursday, his depression bites back with a vengeance. Or, perhaps he feels exhausted from all the work done the previous three days. Whatever the reason, he needs to take a self-care day on Thursday.
